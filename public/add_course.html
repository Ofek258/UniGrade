Fill the form:

To fill out the "Add Course" form: 1. Enter the course name as a string. 2. Specify the credit points as a float (include any decimal values). 3. Select the year 1-7 values. 4. Choose the semester as either "A" or "B". 5. In the table for parts of the grade, enter the name, percent, and grade for each component. Once you've completed all the required fields, submit the form to add the course. Note that the specific form layout may vary.
